Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order from the 'My Orders' section before it is shipped.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery usually takes 3–5 business days depending on your location.

What payment methods do you support?

We support Credit/Debit cards, Bank Transfers, Easypaisa, and Cash on Delivery.

How do I initiate a return?

Go to your Order History, click on the item, and choose 'Return' with the reason.
