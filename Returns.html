Returns & Refunds

At EKart, customer satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not fully satisfied with your purchase, our simple Returns & Refunds policy ensures a smooth experience.

Step 1: Initiate Return

Login to your EKart account and go to "My Orders" to request a return within 7 days of delivery.

Step 2: Product Pickup

Our courier partner will schedule a free pickup from your location.

Step 3: Quality Check

Once the product is received, our team verifies its condition.

Step 4: Refund Processed

After successful verification, your refund is processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Refund Policy Highlights

Full Refund

If the item is damaged, defective, or incorrect, you'll receive a full refund.

Partial Refund

Products returned without all accessories or in non-original packaging may qualify for a partial refund.

No Refund

Items marked as "Final Sale" or used beyond 7 days of delivery are not eligible for a refund.

FAQs

How long do refunds take? +
Refunds usually take 5-7 business days after product verification.
Can I exchange instead of refund? +
Yes, you can opt for an exchange if the product is available in stock.
Are there return charges? +
No, returns are completely free of charge.
