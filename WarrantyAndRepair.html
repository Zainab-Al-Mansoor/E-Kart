Warranty & Repair

At EKart, we stand behind the quality of the products we deliver. Our Warranty & Repair policy ensures that you receive the best post-purchase support.

1. Warranty Coverage

- Most products sold on EKart come with a manufacturer’s warranty ranging from 6 months to 2 years. - Warranty covers manufacturing defects, hardware malfunctions, and product failures under normal use.

2. What is Not Covered

  • Damage caused by misuse, accidents, or unauthorized repairs.
  • Normal wear and tear (scratches, dents, fading).
  • Consumable items such as batteries, cables, and accessories unless specified.

3. Repair Services

- If your product requires repair, EKart will coordinate with authorized service centers. - Repair times may vary depending on the issue, typically ranging from 7 to 21 business days. - Out-of-warranty repairs may incur service and part replacement charges.

⚡ Tip: Always keep your purchase invoice and warranty card safe, as they are required to claim warranty and repair services.

4. How to Claim

To initiate a warranty or repair request:

  • Go to “My Orders” in your EKart account and select the product.
  • Click on “Request Warranty/Repair”.
  • Provide product details, issue description, and attach pictures if required.

5. Contact Us

For warranty or repair-related queries, reach out to our support team:
Email: warranty@ekart.com
Helpline: +1 (800) 987-6543
